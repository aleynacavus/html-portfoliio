About Me

Last year I've graduated form the Faculty of Engineering, department of Biomedical Engineering as the top student.

I graduated with the highest academic standing in the Biomedical Engineering Department, achieving a GPA of 3.47. Furthermore, I developed a watch designed for the hearing impaired to enhance communication and understanding among individuals.

Today I'm working as hearing specialist in a hearing center. Also I'm working on to improve my programming skills. My goal is to improve myself in advanced programming.
